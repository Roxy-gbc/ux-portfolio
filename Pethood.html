iOS App Design | Team Project

PetHood.
Stay aware, ready, and knowledgeable about your pet’s needs.

Context.

When my cat fell ill, I faced difficulties finding a trustworthy vet as an international student in Canada. Through consultations with nearly ten pet owners, I discovered this was a widespread issue. Many struggled to find reliable pet care, and those with established vets often faced appointment delays during emergencies.

Therefore, I took the initiative and designed PetHood under these circumstances. I gathered five interested designers and led the project as the Team Leader and Project Initiator. My roles included conducting user and market research, designing low-fidelity interfaces, prototyping interactions, and testing user flows.

In the end, PetHood was recognized for addressing a crucial need, emphasizing the importance of accessible and reliable pet healthcare.

OVERVIEW

As pet owners, we've all experienced that midnight anxiety when our pets aren't feeling well and we can't find a vet to consult. PetHood was born to solve this problem - an app that helps pet owners connect with veterinarians whenever they need to.

PROBLEM & SOLUTION

Why This Topic?

To be honest, the inspiration came from our own experiences. Almost everyone on our team has pets, and we often find ourselves in our group chat asking: "My dog seems down today, should I take him to the vet?" or "What should I do about my picky-eating cat?"
One day it hit us - hey, why not turn this into our project?

Market Opportunity

The pet care industry has seen significant growth, particularly during the pandemic when pet ownership surged dramatically. We've observed a growing demand for pet healthcare services, yet many people still struggle to access veterinary care when they need it most. This gap in the market presented a clear opportunity for innovation.

Our Solution

PetHood provides a comprehensive platform that enables pet owners to consult veterinarians at any hour, schedule appointments through their phones, and learn from the experiences of other pet owners. This integrated approach addresses the key pain points we identified in the current pet healthcare system.

USER RESEARCH

"How can we help users quickly identify trustworthy veterinarians during urgent situations?"

THE DESIGN PROCESS

Validating Our Direction

Initially, we weren't certain about the exact direction for our project.

Our brainstorming sessions yielded several potential focuses: pet health consultation, pet boarding services, pet-friendly housing information, and a pet owner community platform.

To validate these ideas, we conducted an online survey that garnered over 80 responses. The results were compelling, with 90% of respondents identifying pet health consultation as their primary concern, which strongly validated our chosen direction.

In-Depth Interviews

Following the survey, we conducted detailed interviews with 11 respondents, each lasting 15-20 minutes. These conversations explored their biggest challenges when pets get sick, pain points in accessing veterinary care, and their vision of an ideal pet healthcare service.

USER PERSONAS

Our research revealed three distinct user types:

PERSONA 1

The Nervous Newbie

Typically a first-time pet owner who constantly worries about their pet's wellbeing, feels overwhelmed by online information, and needs reliable guidance.

PERSONA 2

The Experienced Pet Parent

Maintains a relationship with a regular vet but faces availability issues. These users value convenience and reliability, and often struggle when needing to switch veterinarians.

PERSONA 3

The Exotic Pet Owner

Those with hamsters, lizards, and other unusual pets. These owners face particular difficulties in finding knowledgeable veterinarians and reliable information for their specific pets.

COMPETITOR ANALYSIS


Our examination of existing pet health applications in North America revealed significant gaps in service offerings.
Current solutions typically offer limited features, rarely provide emergency consultation services, and almost never cater to exotic pet owners. This analysis confirmed that our comprehensive approach would fill a crucial market need.

IDEATION

Design Process

Through comprehensive user research and market analysis, we identified the most critical user need: instant access to trusted veterinary care, especially during emergencies.

Immediate Professional Support

"Connecting pet owners with trusted vets anytime, anywhere"

Our primary solution enables instant video consultations for emergencies, scheduled check-ups, and maintains comprehensive consultation records for continued care.

Community Knowledge Sharing

"Building a knowledgeable and supportive pet care community"

A curated space where verified veterinarians share expert knowledge and pet owners exchange valuable experiences.

TESTING & ITERATION

User Testing

We conducted thorough usability testing with seven previous interviewees, each dedicating 15-20 minutes to explore the platform. Their testing focused on navigation and feature discovery, the vet booking process, and interaction within the community forum.


Rating System

After careful consideration, we implemented a five-star rating system instead of a simple like/dislike mechanism. This decision provides more detailed vet service feedback, helps users make informed choices, and makes good vets more discoverable.


Navigation Design

We prioritized intuitive navigation by placing the back button consistently in the top-left corner of every screen, making the interface immediately familiar to users while ensuring easy and natural navigation throughout the app.

FINAL DESIGNS

Final Product

Our final design embodies a professional yet welcoming aesthetic that balances urgency with reassurance. The interface prioritizes quick access to emergency care while maintaining intuitive navigation for all features.

Emergency Hotline

A prominent emergency button provides instant access to veterinary care. Users can initiate video or voice calls with just one tap, ensuring immediate assistance during critical situations.

  • One-tap emergency access
  • 24/7 veterinary support
  • Choice of video or voice call

Emergency Chat Consultation

Quick text-based consultations allow pet owners to describe symptoms and share photos with veterinarians, perfect for non-critical situations requiring professional guidance.

  • Real-time messaging with vets
  • Photo sharing capability
  • Secure chat environment

Pet Owner Community

A vibrant community platform where pet owners can share experiences, ask questions, and connect with others. Moderated by veterinary professionals to ensure accurate information.

  • Moderated discussions
  • Expert verification badges
  • Topic-based organization

Key Design Decisions

  • Emergency-First Design: Prominent emergency help button consistently available across all screens
  • Trust Indicators: Comprehensive five-star rating system for veterinarians with detailed reviews
  • Clear Navigation: Intuitive information hierarchy guiding users efficiently to desired actions
LEARNINGS

Key Takeaways

This project revealed several crucial insights about designing for emergency healthcare scenarios:

User research challenged our initial assumptions, proving that what we thought users wanted wasn't always what they needed. Early testing proved invaluable for identifying and addressing critical issues efficiently.

Looking ahead, we plan to implement medication reminders, comprehensive pet health records, and deeper integration with local vet clinics. Each enhancement will be guided by our core mission: making quality veterinary care accessible to all pet owners.
